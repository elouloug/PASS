Practice areas  /  Project & Program Management

Senior delivery leadership, in the chair.

High-stakes projects need senior project managers — people who can hold scope, run vendors, manage stakeholders, and own the outcome. PASS provides delivery leadership for the initiatives where success is not optional.

Senior PM as a service

Most organisations do not have a bench of senior delivery leaders sitting idle. When a strategically important project lands — an ERP rollout, a cloud migration, a regulatory deadline, a digital launch — the internal capacity to lead it well is rarely available, and rarely the right shape.

PASS provides the missing piece: experienced delivery leadership, embedded into the client team for the duration of the initiative. We sit in the project manager's chair — chairing the cadence, owning the plan, managing the vendor, and carrying the outcome — rather than advising from the side.

We work across individual projects and broader programmes, and our engagements span the full lifecycle: scoping, mobilisation, delivery, go-live, hypercare, and benefits realisation.

Where we deliver

Our project and programme leadership work spans the technology and business change initiatives where execution risk is highest and senior delivery experience matters most.

i.
Core systems & ERP
Leading enterprise system implementations and replacements — finance, HR, supply chain, and operational platforms. From requirements through SI selection, configuration, data migration, testing, cutover, and stabilisation.
ii.
Infrastructure & cloud migrations
Data centre exits, cloud platform migrations, network re-architecture, and identity transformations. Programmes that touch every business application and that depend on disciplined sequencing, vendor management, and downtime control.
iii.
Application rollouts
CRM, ITSM, collaboration, and line-of-business application implementations — including the change management, training, and process redesign work that determines whether the new system actually gets used.
iv.
Cyber & compliance programmes
Cybersecurity uplift programmes, regulatory remediation, and audit-driven change initiatives. Programmes where the scope is technical, the stakeholders are senior, and the deadline is fixed.
v.
Digital initiatives
Data platform builds, customer-facing technology launches, automation programmes, and AI-enabled change initiatives — delivered with the same delivery discipline as traditional IT, calibrated for faster cycles and iterative scope.

Our approach

Three principles that distinguish a PASS delivery lead from a contractor with a project plan.

i.

Senior in the chair

Our project leads are experienced practitioners with the judgement to make calls, the credibility to hold stakeholders to commitments, and the technical fluency to challenge the vendor when it matters.

ii.

Vendor management with teeth

System integrators and software vendors are partners, not customers. We hold them to the contract, the plan, and the quality bar — and we know what good looks like from the inside of those firms.

iii.

Through to benefits

Go-live is a milestone, not the finish line. We design engagements to carry through hypercare, adoption, and benefits realisation — the period when many projects quietly lose the value they were chartered to create.
